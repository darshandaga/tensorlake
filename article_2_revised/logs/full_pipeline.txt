---

## 2. Main Trend Direction & Growth Rate

Revenue is on a **confirmed, statistically significant upward trend** (p < 0.0001, R² = 0.82), advancing at approximately **$10–11 per day**. Weekly cohort averages illustrate steady progression: **Week 1 ($1,014) → Week 2 ($1,115) → Week 3 ($1,182) → Week 4 ($1,220)**. However, the rate of weekly incremental gain is decelerating materially — from **+$101 (Week 1→2) to +$66 (Week 2→3) to +$38 (Week 3→4)** — signalling a transition from early-stage acceleration toward a more linear or plateau trajectory. The most recent 7-day rolling average of **$1,852** outpaces all four weekly cohort averages, confirming late-period momentum remains intact despite deceleration in growth rate.

---

## 3. Most Significant Anomalies

Anomaly activity is minimal. The sole flagged data point is **Day 45 ($2,313)**, identified as a Z-score outlier — elevated relative to the dataset mean, though technically within the IQR upper bound of **$2,409**. This spike is most likely attributable to a **promotional event, end-of-month purchasing surge, or a single large enterprise order**. Critically, no IQR-based extreme outliers exist in either direction, and the dataset shows no catastrophic drops or data integrity concerns. The overall signal is one of a **stable, healthy revenue series**.
